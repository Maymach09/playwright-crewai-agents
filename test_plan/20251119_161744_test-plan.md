# Application - Feature Test Plan
## Application Overview
The Salesforce application allows users to manage accounts, contacts, and other business-related data. This test plan focuses on the Accounts module, specifically the creation of a new account with minimum required data.

## Test Scenarios
### 1. Create a New Account
**Seed:** tests/seed.spec.ts

#### 1.1 Happy Path - Create Account with Required Fields
**Steps:**
1. Navigate to the Salesforce Accounts module.
   - Action: Click on "Accounts" [ref=e1403]
   - Expected Result: The Accounts page is displayed with a list of accounts.
   
2. Click on the "New" button to create a new account.
   - Action: Click on "New" [ref=e1012]
   - Expected Result: The New Account form is displayed.

3. Fill in the "Account Name" field.
   - Action: Type "Test Account 3" into the "Account Name" textbox [ref=e1107]
   - Expected Result: The "Account Name" field is populated with "Test Account 3".

4. Fill in the "Phone" field.
   - Action: Type "123-456-7890" into the "Phone" textbox [ref=e1217]
   - Expected Result: The "Phone" field is populated with "123-456-7890".

5. Click on the "Save" button to create the account.
   - Action: Click on "Save" [ref=e1376]
   - Expected Result: A success notification appears, and the new account "Test Account 3" is created.

#### 1.2 Edge Case - Missing Required Fields
**Steps:**
1. Navigate to the Salesforce Accounts module.
   - Action: Click on "Accounts" [ref=e1403]
   - Expected Result: The Accounts page is displayed with a list of accounts.

2. Click on the "New" button to create a new account.
   - Action: Click on "New" [ref=e1012]
   - Expected Result: The New Account form is displayed.

3. Leave the "Account Name" field empty and click on "Save".
   - Action: Click on "Save" [ref=e1376]
   - Expected Result: An error message is displayed indicating that the "Account Name" is required.

4. Fill in the "Account Name" field and click on "Save".
   - Action: Type "Test Account 4" into the "Account Name" textbox [ref=e1107]
   - Expected Result: The "Account Name" field is populated with "Test Account 4".

5. Click on the "Save" button to create the account.
   - Action: Click on "Save" [ref=e1376]
   - Expected Result: A success notification appears, and the new account "Test Account 4" is created.

### Summary
This test plan outlines the steps to create a new account in Salesforce, including both happy path and edge case scenarios. Each step includes specific element details and expected results to ensure clarity for automation engineers.
